#### Understanding Parcel Status

| Status | Meaning | What's Next |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Awaiting Label** | Order created, waiting for merchant | Merchant will prepare parcel |
| **Ready to Ship** | Packed and ready | Waiting for courier pickup |
| **En Route to Distributor** | Courier picked up, going to warehouse | Will arrive at distribution center |
| **At Distributor's Warehouse** | At distribution center | Being sorted for final delivery |
| **Out for Delivery** | Courier is on the way to you | Prepare to receive |
| **Delivered** | Successfully delivered | Rate your experience |
| **Returned** | Could not deliver, returned to merchant | Contact merchant |
| **Cancelled** | Order cancelled | - |

## Workflows

### Workflow 1: Customer Places Order

**Steps:**

1. **Customer** creates delivery request
2. **System** notifies merchant
3. **Merchant** creates parcel with details
4. **System** assigns barcode
5. **Manager/Admin** assigns parcel to courier
6. **Courier** receives notification
7. **Courier** picks up parcel from merchant
8. **Courier** updates status: "Picked Up"
9. **Customer** receives notification
10. **Courier** delivers to warehouse (if applicable)
11. **Courier** updates status: "At Warehouse"
12. **Courier** heads to customer location
13. **Courier** updates status: "Out for Delivery"
14. **Customer** tracks in real-time
